How to Play PokeSim

Your guide to becoming a Pokémon Master – catch, train, and battle your way to the top!

Map Exploration

PokeSim features a 50×50 tile grid where you can explore freely. Use the arrow keys or the on‑screen buttons to move your trainer.

  • Dirt tiles – Safe to walk on, no encounters.
  • Tall grass – Each step has a chance to trigger a random wild Pokémon encounter!
Tip: Explore different areas – every wild Pokémon has a chance to appear, from common to legendary!

Battles & Type Effectiveness

When you encounter a wild Pokémon, a turn‑based battle begins. You can choose from your active Pokémon's moves to attack.

Type effectiveness plays a huge role – using the right move can double your damage, while a wrong move might deal half damage or even none!

Fire Water Grass Electric Psychic Fighting Dark Dragon Fairy Poison Ground Flying Rock Bug Ice Ghost Steel Normal
Tip: Check the type badges in battle – they'll help you choose the most effective move!

Catching Pokémon

When a wild Pokémon is weakened, you can attempt to catch it. The lower its HP, the higher your catch chance!

  • Catching cost: 20% of your active Pokémon's current XP – a risk‑reward decision.
  • Auto‑capture: If you defeat the same species 5 times, the 5th encounter automatically adds it to your collection!
Tip: Weaken the wild Pokémon as much as possible before trying to catch it – but be careful not to faint it!

Leveling & Evolution

Winning battles gives your Pokémon XP. When you gain enough XP, your Pokémon levels up, increasing its stats and HP.

When a Pokémon reaches a certain level, it may evolve automatically into a stronger form! Evolution also updates the Pokémon's moves.

Tip: Keep battling to evolve your Pokémon – they become much stronger after evolution!

Coins & Move Customization

Every battle victory earns you coins (wild level × 5). Coins can be spent to edit your Pokémon's moves.

  • Cost calculation: You only pay for the added power – if you replace a move with a stronger one, you pay the difference (power × 35).
  • No refunds: Reducing power doesn't cost anything, but you don't get coins back either.
  • You can select up to 4 moves per Pokémon from a list of all damaging moves.
Tip: Save your coins for powerful moves – they can make a huge difference in battles!

Team Management

Your team can hold up to 6 Pokémon. You can set an active Pokémon (the one that appears in battles), edit moves, move Pokémon to your collection, or release them.

  • Collection: All your caught Pokémon that aren't in your team. You can search by name or ID, and sort by level, ID, or name.
  • Active Pokémon: The one that leads your team in battles.
Tip: Keep your team balanced with different types to handle any opponent!

Records & Ranking

Records (Pokédex): See every Pokémon you've encountered, with details like type, generation, and encounter count. Search and browse your collection.

Ranking: Compare yourself with other trainers! Rankings are based on total levels of all Pokémon (team + collection) and total encounters.

Tip: The more you battle and catch, the higher you'll climb in the rankings!

Play Anywhere – Install PokeSim

PokeSim is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can:

  • Play instantly in your browser on any device.
  • Install it to your phone's home screen for an app‑like experience.
  • Play offline after installation – your progress syncs when you're back online.
Tip: Look for the "Install App" button in the About section or footer to add PokeSim to your device!
