Map & Exploration

PokeSim features a 50×50 tile map where you roam freely as your trainer. Move using the on-screen arrow pad or your keyboard.

  • Dirt tiles — Safe ground, no wild encounters.
  • Tall grass tiles — Every step has a chance to trigger a wild Pokémon encounter!

Battles & Type Effectiveness

Walking through tall grass triggers a wild Pokémon encounter. A turn-based battle begins — choose one of your moves each turn.

Speed determines who attacks first. A Pokémon's level acts as its base speed (modified by Bonus Stats).

Type effectiveness:

2× Super Effective — double damage
0.5× Not Very Effective — half
0× No Effect
NormalFireWaterElectricGrassIceFightingPoisonGroundFlyingPsychicBugRockGhostDragonDarkSteelFairy

Battle actions:

  • Move buttons (or keys 1–4) — Use one of up to 4 moves.
  • 🎯 Catch — Attempt to catch the wild Pokémon.
  • 🔄 Switch Pokémon — Swap to another team member. Cancelling gives the wild Pokémon a free attack.
  • 🏃 Takas Na! (Escape) — Flee. Failure means the wild Pokémon attacks.
ℹ️ Multi-Pokémon XP: All Pokémon that entered battle via switching share XP. Weaker Pokémon receive a larger share.
💡 Tip: Check Weaknesses in the encounter preview before battle — plan your moves accordingly!

Catching Pokémon

Weakening a wild Pokémon increases your catch chance. The lower its HP, the higher the rate — up to the map's catch cap.

ConditionEffect
🔴 Active Pokémon level ≤ 10Cannot catch — need a stronger Pokémon
📊 XP below map thresholdMust reach required XP % first
💰 Catch costDeducts a % of your active Pokémon's current XP
❤️ Wild HP at ~1%Highest catch chance (up to map's cap)
❤️ Wild HP at 100%Minimum 5% catch chance

Auto-Capture (5th Encounter): Defeat the same species 5 times and on the 5th victory it automatically joins your collection — free, no cost!

⚠️ Important: A failed catch still costs XP and the wild Pokémon gets a free counter-attack. Weaken it first!
💡 Tip: The catch chance display shows the exact % and XP cost in real time above the Catch button.

Leveling & Experience

Defeating wild Pokémon earns XP for all participants. XP is weighted — weaker Pokémon relative to the wild one earn a bigger share.

  • Base XP per battle: Wild level × 30
  • Coins earned: Wild level × 10 per win
  • Max HP: Level × 25
  • XP to next level: Current level × 300
ℹ️ Total Level (Trainer Rank): Sum of all Pokémon levels in team + collection. Used for Global Ranking and unlocking the 🛒 Merkado at total level 100.
💡 Tip: Switch in lower-level Pokémon during battle so they share XP and grow faster.

✨ Shiny Pokémon

Every wild Pokémon has a tiny chance of appearing as a Shiny — an alternate coloration. Purely cosmetic but highly prized.

✨ SHINY  1 in 4,096 chance per encounter
  • Shiny Pokémon display a ✨ SHINY badge on their card.
  • In battle they use alternate shiny sprites (animated or official artwork).
  • Shiny status is preserved through evolution.
  • The encounter preview announces ✨ SHINY [NAME]! when you find one.
💡 Tip: Even if you already own that species, catch the shiny — it's stored separately in your collection!

Move Editor & Customization

Each Pokémon can have up to 4 damaging moves. Customize via the ✏️ Moves button on any card.

  • Cost formula: Added Power × 35 coins. You only pay for added total power.
  • Replacing weaker with stronger costs the power difference × 35.
  • Reducing power or no net change — no cost (no refunds).

💡 Tip: Type coverage (moves of different types) lets you hit more opponents for super effective damage.

Collection Filters & Sorting:

  • 🔍 Search by name or Pokémon #
  • ✨ Filter: Shiny only
  • ⚔️ / 🛡️ / 🏃 Filter: Bonus ATK / DEF / SPD only
  • Sort by: Level (High→Low / Low→High), Pokédex ID, or Name A→Z

Each card shows capture metadata: trainer who caught it, map, and date.

💡 Tip: Keep at least 2–3 Pokémon in your team so you can switch during battle. If all faint, you lose!

Pokédex Records

Tracks every species you've encountered in battle — even without catching. Each entry shows the sprite, name, Pokédex #, types, encounter count, and an ℹ️ Info button with full stats, description, weaknesses, and the complete evolution line.

Search by name or ID. List loads 25 at a time for performance.

💡 Tip: Once you've encountered a species 5 times, the next defeat auto-captures it for free!

Global Ranking

The Global Ranking page compares all trainers by:

  • Total Levels — Sum of all Pokémon levels (team + collection).
  • Total Encounters — Total wild Pokémon battled.

Change your trainer name using the ✏️ button in the nav — once per week.

ℹ️ Merkado Unlock: Reach a Total Level of 100 to unlock the 🛒 Merkado in the navigation bar.

Install PokeSim as an App

PokeSim is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Install to your phone or desktop — no app store needed.

  • Android / Desktop Chrome: Tap "Install App" in the About section or footer.
  • iOS (Safari): Share button → Add to Home Screen.
  • Offline play: Many features work offline. Progress syncs on reconnect.
  • Updates: Use "Check for update" in About to get the latest version.
💡 Tip: Installed app gives full-screen mode, faster loads, and works without keeping a browser tab open.
